Labor Union Relief Funds

The Labor Movement Relief Funds is advertised by the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations, which is an organization composed of more than 10 millon union workers. Donations to any of the listed non-profits, unions, or establishments are used as a means to support individuals that have been devastated by the COVID-19 pandemic. If this cause interests you, make sure to check them out!

Save the Children

There are disparities throughout the country that have stopped people from reaching financial security. Therefore, in order to combat wealth inequality, people continue to donate to poor and marginalized communities. By doing so, education and resources in those areas can improve and children will have a better opportunity to follow their dreams. If Save the Children is a cause that interests you, make sure to check them out!
